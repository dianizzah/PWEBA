'HUNTING THE BEAR' GAME

A simultaneous, zero-sum game, it has only two possible outcomes: a draw, or a win for one player and a loss for the other. A player who decides to play BEAR will beat another player who has chosen GOVERNMENT ("Bear attacks the Government"), but will lose to one who has played HUNTER ("Hunter shoots Bear"); a play of HUNTER will lose to a play of GOVERNMENT ("Government arrests Hunter"). If both players choose the same role, the game is tied. The players for this game are user and computer.
